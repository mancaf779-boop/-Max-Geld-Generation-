# 06 — Personal Brand Statement Builder

**Category:** Career & Personal
**Difficulty:** Beginner
**Best for:** Professionals, founders, freelancers, job seekers
**Source inspiration:** Widely used across LinkedIn coaching, personal branding,
and career development communities

---

## Prompt

```
You are a personal branding expert and career strategist who helps professionals articulate their unique value.

Help me write my personal brand statement.

**My name:** [YOUR NAME]
**Current role / title:** [WHAT YOU DO NOW]
**Target audience:** [WHO YOU WANT TO REACH — employers, clients, collaborators]
**What I'm known for (or want to be known for):** [YOUR CORE EXPERTISE OR APPROACH]
**My unique background or perspective:** [WHAT MAKES YOUR PATH UNUSUAL OR INTERESTING?]
**My values:** [3 CORE VALUES]
**What I want people to do after encountering my brand:** [e.g., hire me / follow me / reach out]

Create:
1. **One-line personal tagline** (under 15 words) — punchy and memorable
2. **LinkedIn headline** (under 220 characters) — includes role, value, and differentiation
3. **Short bio** (2-3 sentences) — for social profiles, speaker bios, portfolios
4. **Extended bio** (1 paragraph, ~100 words) — for website About pages
5. **Elevator pitch script** (30-second spoken version) — natural, not stiff
6. **LinkedIn "About" section** (3-4 paragraphs) — story-driven, first person, ends with CTA

For each version, explain what strategic choice you made in the positioning.
```

---

## Tips

- Include 2-3 testimonials or compliments you've received — they reveal how
  others perceive your value
- Ask Claude to identify the "niche within a niche" where you'd stand out most
  distinctly

---

## Anwendungs-Richtlinie (für Claude in diesem Repo)

Wenn der Nutzer diesen Modus auslöst ("hilf mir mit meiner Personal Brand",
"schreib meine Bio / mein LinkedIn-Headline"):

1. **Profil-Felder erfragen oder annehmen** — Name, Rolle, Zielgruppe,
   Positionierung, besonderer Hintergrund, 3 Werte, gewünschte Handlung.
2. **Alle sechs Formate liefern:** Tagline, LinkedIn-Headline, Kurz-Bio,
   erweiterte Bio, Elevator-Pitch (gesprochen, natürlich), LinkedIn-„About".
3. **Pro Version die strategische Positionierungs-Entscheidung** kurz erklären.
4. **Authentisch statt aufgeblasen** — keine Buzzword-Hülsen; die „Nische in der
   Nische" herausarbeiten, wo die Person am klarsten heraussticht.
5. Sprache an den Nutzer anpassen (hier meist Deutsch); Längen-/Zeichenlimits
   einhalten.
